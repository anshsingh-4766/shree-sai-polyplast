About Us

Shree Sai Polyplast is a trusted name in the plastic manufacturing industry, dedicated to delivering high-quality plastic packaging materials to a wide array of industries. Established in 1992 with a vision to provide innovative and reliable plastic solutions, our company has grown into a reputable manufacturer and supplier.

Our facility, located in Silvassa, is equipped with modern machinery and an experienced workforce that ensures the production of durable and cost-effective plastic products. Our product range includes plastic carry bags, sheets, , and customized packaging solutions to meet specific client needs.

At Shree Sai Polyplast, quality and customer satisfaction are our core values. We work closely with suppliers and clients to maintain consistency and improve with every batch we produce.

With a commitment to innovation and eco-conscious manufacturing practices, we strive to make a positive impact in the packaging industry.
